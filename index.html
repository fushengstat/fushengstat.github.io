About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I also completed a year of joint Ph.D. training at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Following my Ph.D., I served as a research fellow at the National University of Singapore.

[News] I will be attending the ICSA 2023 Applied Statistics Symposium in Ann Arbor, Michigan, in June.

Research Interests

  • Statistical Machine Learning: classification/regression, high-dimensional statistics, kernel method, statistical learning theory, robust statistics.

  • Biostaistics: case-control study, integration analysis, summary statistics.

  • Genetics: genome-wide association study, subtype heterogeneity analysis.

  • Optimization: algorithm, (non-)convex optimization, online learning, robust optimization.
