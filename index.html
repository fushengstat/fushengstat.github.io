Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am an Associate Professor at Nankai University. Prior to this position, I completed a Postdoctoral training at both the National Institutes of Health and the National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I also completed a year of joint Ph.D. training at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. If you're interested in my research and open to potential collaborations in statistics and its applications, please don't hesitate to contact me by email.
