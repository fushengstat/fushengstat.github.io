Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the National Institutes of Health. I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I also completed a year of joint Ph.D. training at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Following my Ph.D., I served as a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore. If you're interested in my research and open to potential collaborations in statistics and its applications, please don't hesitate to contact me by email.
